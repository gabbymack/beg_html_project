EVOLVING, CHEF-DRIVEN & LOCALLY-OWNED

Moi, Cherie! is an evolving chef-driven vegetable, fruit, and active-foods restaurant in San Diego, CA. We serve dinner from an open kitchen and bar, with 10 tables, 40 seats, kitchen-side dining, and a patio.

Chef Molly Gabbard opened Moi, Cherie! in the summer of 2021. Since then, Moi, Cherie! has achieved a place among peers in the San Diego food scene. Moi, Cherie! has been voted best vegetarian restaurant in the city, healthiest bite, greenest restaurant, best service, and among the most romantic places to dine. Moi, Cherie! has garnered attention nationally, making lists from Zagat, Food and Wine, and PETA.


"We cook for enjoyment, and we eat deliciously replenish. Our aspiration is to redefine expectations for vegan restaurant food. Our menu is small so that we can remake it daily. The food you see and smell before you was prepared by the chefs that you see in the kitchen, on the spot daily."
– chef Molly Gabbard

Moi, Cherie! is a dedicated member of the Plant Based Food Association. Visit their website for more information on how to become a certified vendor in the national plant based industry.
